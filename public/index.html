About Banasthali_OLX

Designed exclusively for the proud students of Banasthali Vidyapith, our platform bridges the gap between seniors and juniors, creating a sustainable and affordable ecosystem within the campus walls.

Whether you are moving out of your hostel and need to sell your cycle, or you are a fresher looking for affordable course books and lab coats, Banasthali_OLX is your one-stop destination.
